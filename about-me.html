My name is John and living the simple life saved my life

I used to be a park rat. I had so many things i thought i loved. But the truth was that I couldn't even appreciate any of it because there was to much.

Things were terrible. I had a job I hated, a home that I didn't want to live in, and I couldn't hold a relationship.

How I turned things around

I was on verge of a breakdown when I got rid of everything. I lterally put 90% of all my belongings in the garbage, sold my place and OI moved into a smaller apartment, and I quit my job.

It wasn't easy at all, but everything around me was in a bad place, I didn't know what else to do. And it worked.

Now I live the simple life

Now that I'm living a simple life, things are so much better. I'm less stressed., enjoy life and work more, and I have more free time to enjoy

With how much it turned my life around, I felt like I have no choice but to start sharing how i did it, and how others can benefit from living the simple life as well, which is why I started this site!
